Homeopathic training in South Africa involves a five year full-time degree (MHSc in Homeopathy at DUT or BHSc CM at UJ). Registration with the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa (AHPCSA) is a statutory requirement and graduates become eligible for registration upon completion of their academic and statutory requirements. It is only offered in 2 universities:
