Medical Laboratory Science

All About Medical Laboratory Science

Medical Laboratory Science (MLS) is a field that studies the analysis of human fluids, tissues, and cells to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases. Medical Laboratory Scientists, also known as Medical Technologists, play a vital role in healthcare by providing crucial information to doctors about a patient's health.

Being a Medical Laboratory Scientist is my dream. I discovered the career last year and I was hooked. I think Medical Laboratory Scientists are the back-bones of health, they are the reason why your doctor is able to diagnose your illness. When I study the course I'll specialize in Clinical Pathology. Clinical Pathology is a combination of Chemical Pathology, Haematology and Microbiology.

Medical lab scientists are called the Oracle of modern medicine because we might not be seen by patients daily in most health facilities but they play a vital role in the diagnosis of diseases and patients' recovery.

In South Africa Med lab is offered as a 4 years Honours degree (NQF level 8), Bachelor of Health Sciences in Medical Laboratory Sciences. It is offered in the following universities:

  • Durban University of Technology.
  • University of Johannesburg.
  • Tshwane University of Technology.
  • Cape Peninsula University of Technology.
  • Nelson Mandela University.
  • Mangosuthu University of Technology.

PathCare also offers a Student Medical Laboratory Technician (SMLT) programme which is a two-year programme in which suitable candidates are recruited to undergo training to become Medical Laboratory Technicians.
